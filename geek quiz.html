Geek Quiz - Technical Quiz


UNLOCK YOUR TECH IQ

Vision: To be the premier technical quiz event that not only tests but expands the horizons of knowledge in the fields of science and technology. We envision GEEKWIZ as a catalyst for continuous learning, inspiring future leaders and innovators to push the boundaries of what's possible in the technological realm



Mission: “To ignite curiosity, foster learning, and challenge the intellect of participants through engaging and thought-provoking technical quizzes. GEEKWIZ aims to create an inclusive platform where enthusiasts can showcase their knowledge, stimulate critical thinking, and celebrate the spirit of innovation and technology”



Description:GeekWiz is a technical quiz where students Dive Into The World of Technologies. Participants in the GeekWiz event not only showcased their existing knowledge but also gained new insights and expanded their understanding through the challenging questions and discussions. The Motive Behind Our GeekWiz ➢ Stimulating Minds ➢ Cultivating Knowledge
